Who Do I Talk To?

It's important to know that lots of children suffer from abuse. There are lots of scary things that might be going on in your family. Maybe your mum yells at you and calls you bad names, or maybe your dad leaves you out of things and forgets that it is important to think about you with some things. No matter what might be going on at home you are not alone. It is okay to speak to people about what you are dealing with. Remember that if something is upsetting you and you want help then don't be afraid to ask them.
